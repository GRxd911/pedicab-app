Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Dumaguete Pedicab App. By using this platform, you agree to follow the rules set by the Traffic Management Office (TMO) and the city government of Dumaguete.

2. User Safety

Safety is our top priority. Passengers must behave respectfully, and Drivers must maintain their vehicles in good condition. Any form of harassment or unsafe driving will result in an immediate permanent ban.

3. Fares & Payments

Fares are calculated based on the standard rates approved by the city. Passengers are expected to pay the amount shown on the app. No overcharging is allowed.

4. Data Privacy

We respect your privacy. Your location data is only used to connect Passengers with Drivers and is never shared with 3rd parties. We follow the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012.

5. Driver Fatigue Policy

To ensure road safety, Drivers are required to take mandatory 15-minute breaks after 4 hours of continuous service. The app will automatically send reminders for your well-being.
